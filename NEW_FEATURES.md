## üéØ **How to Use:**

### **For Brand Owners:**

1. **Edit Your Campaign:**
   - Go to your campaign page
   - Click "Edit Campaign" button (top right)
   - Update details
   - Click "Update Campaign"

2. **Delete Your Campaign:**
   - Go to your campaign page
   - Click "Delete" button (top right, red)
   - Confirm deletion
   - Campaign and all products removed

3. **Activate/Deactivate Campaign:**
   - Go to your campaign page
   - Click "Activate" or "Deactivate" button
   - Campaign visibility toggles

4. **Manage Products:**
   - Go to your campaign page
   - Each product has Edit and Delete buttons
   - Click Edit to modify product
   - Click Delete to remove from campaign
